About me

Incredibly I’ve been living in Spain for almost a decade now and I’m learning new things about my adopted country every day. I love the food here and in Barcelona I’ve found the perfect combination of fantastic city (with a thriving tech. scene), spectacular coastlines and nearby, breathtaking mountain ranges. I love this city.

I also love technology and literally learn something new everyday given how rapidly new tech. is developed. Recently I have been trying to get my head around web3 and blockchain technology, particularly Solana.

Experience

My first passion was teaching. In 2011 in Greenwich, London I trained as an ESL teacher and then embarked on my career as an English as a Second Language teacher in South America. I arrived in Spain about a year later and started giving language training in some of the biggest companies on the planet.

I loved all my students but the classes I had in both start-ups and big tech. companies with groups of developers were always the most exciting for me and sparked something in me that inspired me to learn to code.

Skills

Javascript is the language I am using everyday. Ubiquitous, versatile and ever evolving. Who doesn’t love Javascript?

Although a fickle and occasionally infuriating beast, I do love the process of going from a totally blank screen to a fully styled webpage - even if that div is slightly off centre for some inexplicable reason.

And where else to put all the style and JS scripts you've spent hours writing if not inside some good old HTML.

React was the first framework (ok, ok, library) I really fell in love with. Although I’m familiar with Ruby on Rails, React is where things really started to click for me. Marrying all the aspects of Javascript, CSS and HTML that I had learnt and putting them all together in a dynamic, intuitive system for building out entire web apps. Very cool.
